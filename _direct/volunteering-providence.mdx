<p> Volunteering means making a regular commitment. We ask our volunteers to commit to one of the following placement choices: </p>
<ul>
    <li> A minimum of one shift (approx. 3-4 hours) for six months and 75 hours for our regular program for both adults and youth. </li>
    <li> A minimum of one shift (2 hours) per week for six months assisting with meals in Providence Hospital or the Houses of Providence, our Long-Term Care Home. </li>
    <li> The minimum time commitment for co-op placements is determined by the high school, university or college. </li>
    <li> Summer Youth volunteers are required to complete 80 hours that consist of 4 hours/day, 5 days/week for 4 weeks in July or August </li>
</ul>
