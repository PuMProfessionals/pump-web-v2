<p> Volunteers play an active and important role at Chartwell, and we are always looking for caring and compassionate people who are eager to make a difference. 
Whether you are interested in assisting our trained staff in a specific area, or leading a program or activity on your own, we can help customize your experience 
so that it meets your needs while positively impacting the lives of our residents. </p>

<p> If you are looking for meaningful ways to contribute your skills and/or time toward helping seniors in your community, we have multiple opportunities available. 
With over 175 residences across Canada, we likely have a home in your neighbourhood. </p>
