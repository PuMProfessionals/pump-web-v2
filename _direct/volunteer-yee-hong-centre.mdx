<p> Volunteering for Yee Hong is a fulfilling and rewarding experience. Our volunteers embrace the seven 
values of Yee Hong: Respect, Compassion, Commitment, Excellence, Teamwork, Integrity and Accountability. 
Whether conducting programs, visiting seniors, delivering meals, transporting seniors or doing office work, 
their service helps our seniors enjoy a more fulfilling life. With the contribution of over 1,000 service 
volunteers every year, Yee Hong is proud to be a world leader in providing quality services for seniors, 
from the healthy to the very frail. </p>

<h3> Requirements: </h3>
<ul>
    <li> Must be 16 or older </li>
    <li> Tubercuolosis screening </li>
</ul>
